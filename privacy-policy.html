Privacy Policy for MaskMagic2

Last Updated: June 15, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use MaskMagic2 ("the App").

Information We Collect

MaskMagic2 is designed to respect your privacy. We collect minimal information required for the app to function:

  • Images: When you upload or create images for editing in the app, these images are processed locally on your device.
  • Device Information: For diagnostic purposes only, we may collect basic information about your device (model, iOS version) to help troubleshoot issues.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process your images for AI-based editing and generation
  • Diagnose technical issues with the app
  • Improve the app's performance and user experience

External Services

MaskMagic2 uses the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI API: When you use the image generation feature, your processed images and text prompts are sent to OpenAI's servers via a secure Google Cloud Function. This is necessary to generate AI-powered image edits.
  • Google Cloud Functions: We use Google Cloud Functions as a secure intermediary to process your requests to the OpenAI API. This protects our API credentials and enhances security.

Data Sharing and Transfer

Your data is shared only with the third-party services mentioned above, solely for the purpose of providing the app's functionality. We do not sell, rent, or otherwise share your personal information with other third parties.

Data Retention

Images and prompts sent to OpenAI are subject to their data retention policies. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for more information.

Any data stored locally on your device remains there until you delete the app or clear its data.

User Rights

As a user of MaskMagic2, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we process about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to our processing of your data

Children's Privacy

MaskMagic2 is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub.
